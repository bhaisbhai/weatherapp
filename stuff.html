LONDON CALLING! 🇬🇧 EVENTS & HIDDEN GEMS

Battersea Beat Check-In

Explore your (potential) local patch! Stroll through Battersea Park, check out the Power Station (shops, restaurants, events!), and see what local markets or gatherings are on via "Battersea Beat".

YT: Battersea Vibes ➡️

Secret London & Time Out Recommends

Dive into their apps/websites for May 17-18! Look for unique pop-ups, food festivals (vegan options!), outdoor art, quirky markets, or free walking tours.

Search YouTube based on what you find! E.g., "[Specific Market Name] London review"

Good News IRL (Inspired by TanksGoodNews)

Seek out community events, charity pop-ups, or peaceful spots like the Chelsea Physic Garden (entry fee applies) for positive vibes.

FOODIE FOCUS! 🍣🌱 WINGS, BURGERS & VEGAN!

Your Deliveroo history is a MOOD (187 orders, we stan!). And that "vegan_veganfood" follow? We see you! Let's EAT.

VEGAN VIBES ACROSS LONDON:

Vegan Sushi Fix

Try Stem & Glory or Apres Food Co. for innovative plant-based sushi-inspired dishes.

YT: Vegan Sushi London ➡️

Craving Wings? (Vegan Ed!)

Temple of Seitan is THE go-to. Halo Burger often has great "chicken" style options too.

YT: Temple of Seitan ➡️

Burger Bliss (Plant-Based)

Check out Neat Burger or the colourful Flower Burger for amazing vegan patties.

YT: Neat Burger ➡️

GENERAL VEGAN HOTSPOTS:

Mildreds (Various Locations)

A London veggie/vegan institution. Always delicious and a safe bet!

Unity Diner (Spitalfields)

Non-profit, all-vegan diner with amazing comfort food. Good food for a good cause!

Don't forget your faves like Elements Sushi, Wingstop, or Shake Shack – many now have great vegan options too! And Waitrose for those vegan treats. 🛒

WELLNESS & CHILL 🧘‍♀️🎧 GOOD VIBES!

Tonic Health & Taurbeauty Inspired Wellness

Hit up healthy cafes like Wild Food Cafe or Farmacy. Find mindful moments in a park. Or have a DIY spa sesh with a nice face mask!

YT: Healthy London Cafes ➡️

Podcast & Chill (Your Spotify Faves)

Catch up on "Sleep With Me," "The Best One Yet," "Juicy Couture Tracksuit," etc. Find a comfy spot and relax. Explore playlists of South Asian artists like Aja Mahi or Pittal too!

YT: Chill Desi Beats ➡️

Meme IRL & Positivity

Look for quirky street art, visit a fun exhibition like the Twist Museum, or just people-watch in Covent Garden. Spread those good news vibes!
